👋 Hello, I'm Dr Tom O'Shea |

I am a Senior Analyst for Place at the West of England Mayoral Combined Authority and a researcher working at the intersection of spatial modelling, behavioural analysis and environmental intelligence. I build transparent, reproducible analytical systems that help organisations understand how people interact with their environments and how places evolve under social, spatial and environmental pressures.

🔍 What I work on:

> Spatial and agent‑based modelling

> Gravity and interaction frameworks for greenspace and place‑based behaviour

> Housing, accessibility and land‑use analytics

> Reproducible scientific workflows in R, Quarto and GitHub

> Visual storytelling through maps, figures and spatial narratives

🌱 Current focus:

I develop analytical foundations that support regional planning, housing evidence and place‑based decision-making across the West of England. This has included work on ViSAGE, a flexible modelling framework for simulating greenspace visitation and testing development impacts, alongside broader spatial evidence systems that integrate housing delivery, affordability, accessibility and environmental data.

🧠 Theoretical interests:

> Socio Spatial Perspective (SSP)

> Actor‑Network Theory (ANT)

> Applied Assemblage Theory (AAT)

> Object‑Oriented Ontology (OOO)

These approaches shape how I think about environments, behaviour and the relational structures that underpin spatial systems.

🧰 Skills and interests:

> Rigorous spatial modelling and simulation

> Transparent, modular code design

> Scientific communication and figure sequencing

> Environmental intelligence for everyday lives

> Place‑based analytics and socio-spatial insight

📫 Get in touch:

I’m interested in discussing modelling approaches, spatial evidence development, greenspace accessibility or reproducible analytical workflows.
